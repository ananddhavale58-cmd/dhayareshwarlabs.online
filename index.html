Our Products

AQI Monitoring System

The Air Quality Monitoring System (AQMS) is an advanced, reliable solution designed to continuously monitor ambient air quality in real time. The system accurately measures particulate matter (PM2.5 and PM10) along with key gaseous pollutants to provide precise and actionable air quality data. AQMS is widely used across industrial facilities, construction sites, residential areas, smart city projects, highways, and public spaces. The system ensures continuous data acquisition, storage, and visualization through a cloud-based dashboard, enabling users to monitor air quality remotely via web or mobile platforms.

OCEMS System

The Online Continuous Emission Monitoring System (OCEMS) is a regulatory-compliant solution designed to continuously monitor stack emissions from industrial processes in real time. The system measures critical emission parameters and automatically transmits validated data to the CPCB / SPCB (MPCB) servers as per statutory requirements OCEMS ensures transparent, tamper-resistant, and uninterrupted monitoring of industrial emissions. The system consists of certified gas analyzers, particulate monitors, data acquisition systems (DAS), and secure communication modules to provide accurate and reliable emission data.

LED AQI Display Board

The LED AQI Display Board is a high-visibility digital display solution designed to show real-time Air Quality Index (AQI) and pollutant data for public awareness and regulatory compliance. The display receives live data from AQI Monitoring Systems or cloud servers and presents it in a clear, readable format for outdoor and indoor installations. The LED AQI Display Board is widely installed at industrial gates, construction sites, public places, residential societies, smart city projects, and highways to inform the public about current air quality conditions

Water OCEMS System

The Water OCEMS (Online Continuous Effluent Monitoring System) is an automated, regulatory-compliant solution designed to continuously monitor industrial effluent and wastewater discharge parameters in real time. The system measures key water quality parameters and securely transmits data to CPCB / SPCB (MPCB) servers as per statutory guidelines. Water OCEMS ensures continuous, transparent, and tamper-proof monitoring of treated effluent from industries. The system includes certified online analyzers, sensors, a data acquisition system (DAS), and a secure communication module for accurate data capture and reporting.
